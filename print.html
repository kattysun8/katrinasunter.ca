Rebrand

Description

This project shows the process of rebranding a logo. The first step was to complete background research including competitors and customer personas.

Brainstorm

Sketches Sketches Sketches

The next step involves diving into ideation. Everything that comes to mind was sketched, because it is never known what will come of it.

Choose One

Narrowing it Down

Two of the preliminary sketch concepts with some potential were expanded on.

Wordmark

Choosing the Font

Now that two final concepts have been chosen, the font has to be chosen for each. A variety were experimented, crossing out the ones that did not match the findings from background research.

Colours

What is the goal of the restaurant

Understanding that the aim of the restaurant is to have a fresh, crisp and refreshing look and feel, colours were chosen that share this description.

Refinement

Focusing on the details

Refinement is where a concept can really start to shine visaully. Simple adjustments to the design can make or break the logo.
